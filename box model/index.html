Padding

Padding is a space between conten box and border


Padding shorthand property

To apply to all four sides
padding: 10px;

vertical | horizontal
padding: 10px 10px;

top | horizontal | bottom
padding: 10px 10px 10p 15px;

top | right | bottom | left
padding: 10px 15px 10px 15px;

Margin

margin is the space on the outside, while padding is the space on the inside
